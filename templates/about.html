About

This is a website for various articles of mine, mostly about books and ideas: long-form philosophical essays, literary criticism, and other kinds of analytical writing.

When I’m not writing these articles I run The Philosophy Forum, an online community that I started in 2015. Since then, TPF has grown into a popular website for long-form philosophical discussion. I still take an active role there, moderating discussions, organizing reading groups, and keeping things running behind the scenes.

I'm also a software developer and have spent many years building web applications. I co-founded and built CommissionCrowd.

You can reach me at alistair.robinson@gmail.com.
