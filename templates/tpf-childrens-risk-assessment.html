Online Safety Act: Sample Children's Risk Assessment

Prepared for compliance with the Online Safety Act 2023 (United Kingdom).
Date: March 4th, 2026.

1. Service Description

The Philosophy Forum (TPF) is an online discussion forum dedicated to philosophy and related subjects such as politics, ethics, and social science. The platform allows registered users to create discussions and post replies.

The service is moderated by volunteer moderators and administrators. Community guidelines govern acceptable conduct and content.

The forum is operated from the United Kingdom and is publicly accessible on the web.

2. Access by Children

The forum requires users to confirm that they are at least 13 years old when registering an account.

Children aged 13–17 may therefore access the service.

The forum does not target children as its primary audience and its content is generally oriented toward adult philosophical discussion.

3. Features Relevant to Safety

The platform includes the following features:

  • User-generated posts and discussions
  • Moderation by administrators and moderators
  • A flagging/reporting system allowing users to report problematic posts
  • Private messaging between users
  • Account suspension and banning tools

Community guidelines prohibit harassment, abuse, and other forms of disruptive behaviour.

4. Potential Risks to Children

Possible risks to users aged 13–17 include:

  • Exposure to hostile or uncivil debate
  • Exposure to offensive or controversial language
  • Exposure to political or ideological arguments
  • Harassment or personal attacks by other users
  • Inappropriate use of private messaging

The forum does not host pornography or explicit adult material, but discussions may occasionally involve mature philosophical or political topics.

5. Risk Mitigation Measures

The forum implements the following safeguards:

  • Published community guidelines requiring respectful discussion
  • Active moderation by administrators and moderators
  • The ability for users to flag posts for moderator review
  • The ability for users to flag private messages and direct chat messages for moderator review
  • Moderator powers to remove posts, private messages, and chat messages; and to silence, suspend, delete, and block users
  • Clear Terms of Service including a minimum age requirement (13+)
  • If moderators become aware that a user is under the age of 13, the account will be removed in accordance with the forum’s Terms of Service.

Moderators may intervene where discussions become abusive, hostile, or disruptive.

6. Overall Risk Assessment

The service presents a relatively low risk environment for children compared to many social media platforms because:

  • It is discussion-based rather than algorithmically amplified
  • Content is primarily text-based and intellectual in nature
  • Moderation is active
  • The community has established norms of reasoned debate

The main risks arise from uncivil argument or exposure to controversial topics, which are addressed through moderation and community guidelines.

7. Review

This assessment will be reviewed periodically, particularly if significant changes are made to the platform’s features or moderation systems.
