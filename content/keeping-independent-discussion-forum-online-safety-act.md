## 3. Why I Decided to Migrate the Forum
- Limitations of the previous forum platform
- Difficulty demonstrating adequate moderation capability
- Desire for clearer governance and moderation tools
- Decision to migrate to Discourse

## 4. What Compliance Actually Requires (in Practice)
- Clarifying misconceptions about the law
- Reasonable safeguards rather than perfect policing
- Core practical requirements for a forum
  - Clear terms of service
  - Minimum age policy (13+)
  - Ability to report problematic content
  - Moderation procedures
  - Removal of illegal or harmful content
  - Transparency of moderation actions

## 5. How Discourse Helps with Compliance
- Built-in reporting and flagging system
- Moderation workflow
- Staff logs and auditability
- Trust level system
- Ability to moderate posts, messages, and chat
- Tools for handling abuse and disruptive users

## 6. Additional Governance Measures
- Updated community guidelines
- Moderation handbook for staff
- Child safety risk assessment
- Explicit policy on AI-generated content
- Clear procedures for removing under-13 accounts

## 7. The Technical Migration
- Exporting data from the old forum
- Deploying the new Discourse instance

- Preserving link integrity and search indexing

## 8. Lessons for Other Forum Administrators
- Compliance is manageable with the right tools
- Importance of clear moderation processes
- Value of transparent governance
- Modern forum software significantly reduces compliance risk

## 9. Conclusion
